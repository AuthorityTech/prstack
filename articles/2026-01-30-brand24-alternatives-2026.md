Brand24 is a popular social listening tool, but it's not the only option—and for many startups, it's not the best one. Whether you're concerned about pricing, looking for deeper media monitoring capabilities, or need better AI-powered insights, there are compelling alternatives worth considering.

This comprehensive guide compares the best Brand24 alternatives in 2026, from affordable social monitoring tools to performance-based earned media platforms with AI visibility tracking.

## Featured Alternatives

1. **AuthorityTech** – AI visibility + earned media monitoring (performance-based pricing)
2. **Meltwater** – Enterprise media intelligence ($15k-$50k/year)
3. **Mention** – Affordable social + web monitoring ($41-$149/month)
4. **Critical Mention** – Broadcast + digital monitoring ($2k-$10k/year)
5. **Brandwatch** – Enterprise social listening ($20k+/year)
6. **Sprinklr** – Unified customer experience ($30k+/year)
7. **Awario** – Affordable SMB social listening ($29-$299/month)

**Key insight:** Most brand monitoring tools track social and traditional media, but only AuthorityTech tracks AI search visibility (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini citations).
