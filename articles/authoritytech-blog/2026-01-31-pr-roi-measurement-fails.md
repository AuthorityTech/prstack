73% of CMOs can't measure PR ROI because traditional retainers make it impossible. Performance-based pricing fixes measurement by tying payment to results.

## Why Traditional PR ROI Measurement Fails

Traditional PR agencies charge monthly retainers for "strategy," "outreach," and "monitoring" with no guaranteed outcomes. This pricing structure makes ROI measurement impossible because payment is disconnected from results.

When brands pay $5,000+ per month regardless of outcomes, ROI calculation becomes impossible. Research shows 68% of retainers lack placement metrics entirely. Performance-based pricing fixes this by aligning payment with outcomes: cost per placement divided by placement value.
